Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an account?
To register for an account, you need to use our Telegram bot. Send /register command followed by your customer ID and password. Once registered, you can login to this web portal using the same credentials.
How do I connect my Telegram account?
Open Telegram and search for @YourBotName. Start a conversation with the bot and use the /register command with your customer ID and password. The bot will automatically link your Telegram account to your trading account.
What are the available bot commands?
Available commands include:
  • /start - Start interaction with the bot
  • /register - Register your account
  • /balance - Check your current balance
  • /history - View transaction history
  • /help - Get help and support
How do I reset my password?
For security reasons, password resets must be done through our support team. Contact support via Telegram or email with your customer ID and verification information to reset your password.
How are transactions processed?
Transactions are automatically detected and processed by our bot when you send messages in the designated trading groups. The bot uses pattern recognition to identify deposits, withdrawals, and other transaction types. Your balance is updated in real-time.
What is the minimum deposit/withdrawal amount?
The minimum deposit amount is $100, and the minimum withdrawal amount is $50. These limits are in place to ensure efficient processing of transactions and reduce operational overhead.
